# General Research

Research that isn't tied to a specific project lives here. Think of this as the team's shared knowledge base.

## Examples
- Industry trend analysis
- Technology evaluations
- Literature reviews
- Market research
- Reference material

## Organization
Use subfolders by topic:
```
research/
├── topic-name/
│   ├── findings.md
│   ├── sources.md
│   └── raw-data/
```

When research becomes relevant to a specific POC, copy or link the key findings into that project's `research/` folder.
